The RK Custom Series Dreadnought delivers massive volume and profound bass, thanks to its carefully selected solid Sitka spruce top and mahogany back and sides. Perfect for both aggressive strumming and delicate fingerpicking in the studio or on stage.

SKU: RK-AC-DRD-01

Category: Acoustic Guitars

Tags: Dreadnought, Solid Top, Professional

Built for the Stage

Designed for the working musician, the RK Custom Series Dreadnought is built to project. We hand-select the tonewoods to ensure maximum resonance and structural stability against Kathmandu's varying humidity. The scalloped X-bracing allows the solid spruce top to vibrate freely, resulting in a rich, complex tone that improves with age.

  • Solid Sitka Spruce top for punchy articulation and headroom.
  • Mahogany back and sides for warm, balanced overtones.
  • Satin-finished mahogany neck with a comfortable 'C' profile.
  • Rosewood fretboard with custom RK pearl inlays.
  • Genuine Bone nut and saddle for enhanced sustain.
  • Die-cast high-ratio 18:1 tuning machines for precise tuning.
